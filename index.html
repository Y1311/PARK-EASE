How to Use the Smart Parking System

  1. Create an Account: Register with your Gmail or Yahoo account.
  2. Login: Use your email and strong password.
  3. Check Slots: View available spots on your dashboard.
  4. Reserve a Slot: Book a slot for a preferred time.
  5. Drive to Location: Use map to reach your spot.
  6. License Verification: Your plate is scanned at entry.
  7. Park: Your timer starts when parked.
  8. Pay: Pay via UPI, cards, or wallets.
  9. Logout: Safely exit the website or app.
